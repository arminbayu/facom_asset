How to Withdraw through Exchanger

Here’s the way to Withdraw FasaPay USD through ecurrencyX :

  1. Go to https://www.ecurrencyx.net/ and register or log in to member area
  2. Choose the exchange menu
  3. Fill the exchange data
    • From currency: Choose Fasapay USD
    • To currency: Choose USDT, BTC, PMUSD
    • Click “Exchange” and then you will see the transaction Detail
  1. Click “Pay now/copy address” and then you will transfer your FasaPay balance (USD) to ecurrencyx.net FasaPay account
  2. Follow the next steps based on processing system
